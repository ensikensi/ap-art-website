about me ᯓ★

hi! my name is kensington diefenderfer, and as you know, this is my portfolio. ive been an artist for a long time and i hope to continue that artistic streak throughout my life. i like to make pieces about and inspired by my characters, stories, music, and other media, and i enjoy combining things i enjoy to make one big picture of things i love. i mainly do digital art, but i've grown a love for watercolor and colored pencils!
i like to make a lot of "story" pieces that have emotion and show the story, almost picture book esque, because i make the words and my writing apart of the art, too.

my sustained investigation ⋆˚꩜｡


in the AP art world, you have something called a "sustained investigation", or the 'theme' of your artwork and portfolio. mine is "exploring self-perception and how others percieve you through character design, world-building, and storytelling." i go on to make art, concepts, and designs all centered around this one topic.

"Step 0.5"
Acrylic paint markers, Colored Pencil, Ink pen, 8.5inx11in

The first step of Recovery is acceptance, to accept that you struggle and that you have an addiction. Step 0.5 is knowing you struggle, but not admitting to it. It’s the step you take before you fully commit, and you might be in the limbo of that step for a while before you take the next half step. It’s progress, but not as linear and not as smooth as anyone wants it to be– it's still grueling and even moving half a step forward is a struggle. A step is a step no matter what though, even if you don’t want to admit it.
